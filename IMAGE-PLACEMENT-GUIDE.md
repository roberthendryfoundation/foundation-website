## üé® **Image Recommendations**

### Image Types That Work Well:

1. **People** - Diverse, authentic, supportive interactions
2. **Nature/Calm** - Peaceful, serene scenes (good for mental health context)
3. **Abstract** - Soft, supportive, hopeful imagery
4. **Community** - People working together, supporting each other
5. **Education** - Learning, reading, knowledge sharing

### Image Guidelines:

- **Format:** JPG or WebP (WebP for better compression)
- **Quality:** High quality but optimized (under 200KB per image)
- **Aspect Ratios:**
  - Hero images: 16:9 or 3:2
  - Square images: 1:1 (for profiles, cards)
  - Portrait: 3:4 (for team photos)
- **Colors:** Match your brand colors (primary blues, warm tones)
- **Style:** Professional but approachable, not overly clinical

### Where to Get Images:

- **Unsplash** (free, high-quality): Search "anxiety support", "community", "mental health"
- **Pexels** (free): Similar to Unsplash
- **Your own photos**: Most authentic option
- **Stock photos**: Ensure they're diverse and authentic-looking
